Introduction

NutriSlider ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our fitness calculation app.

Information We Collect and Store

Our app collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Calculator inputs and preferences:
    • Height and weight measurements
    • Age and gender
    • Body measurements (neck, waist, hip)
    • Activity level selections
    • Dietary preferences and goals
  • User preferences:
    • Preferred units (imperial/metric)
    • Selected calculation presets
    • App settings configuration

Local Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device using Apple's AppStorage system. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers. Your data remains on your device and can be cleared by deleting the app.

Advertising

The free version of NutriSlider includes third-party advertisements. Our advertising partners may:

  • Use device identifiers to serve relevant advertisements
  • Collect anonymous usage data for advertising purposes
  • Use tracking technologies to measure advertising effectiveness

Users can opt out of personalized advertising through their device settings or remove ads entirely by purchasing the premium version.

In-App Purchases

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's App Store
  • We do not collect or store any payment information
  • Purchase records are maintained by Apple for receipt validation

Data Security

We protect your information by:

  • Storing all personal data locally on your device
  • Using Apple's secure AppStorage system
  • Not collecting or transmitting data to external servers
  • Regular security updates and maintenance

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age without parent/guardian consultation. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your stored data by deleting the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about your stored data
  • Control app permissions through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
