Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how NutriSlider handles your information. As the developer of NutriSlider, I am committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how it is used.

Information Storage and Collection

NutriSlider stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Calculator inputs and preferences:
    • Height and weight measurements
    • Age and gender
    • Body measurements (neck, waist, hip)
    • Activity level selections
    • Dietary preferences and goals
  • User preferences:
    • Preferred units (imperial/metric)
    • Selected calculation presets
    • App settings configuration

Data Privacy and Storage

All personal data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's AppStorage system. NutriSlider does not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers. Your data remains on your device and can be cleared by deleting the App.

Advertising

The free version of NutriSlider includes third-party advertisements. The advertising partners may:

  • Use device identifiers to serve relevant advertisements
  • Collect anonymous usage data for advertising purposes
  • Use tracking technologies to measure advertising effectiveness

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings or remove ads by purchasing the premium version.

In-App Purchases

For in-app purchases:

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple's App Store
  • NutriSlider does not collect or store any payment information
  • Purchase records are maintained by Apple for receipt validation

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Delete personal information held by businesses
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising their CCPA rights

As NutriSlider stores all data locally on your device and does not collect or sell personal information, many CCPA requirements are not applicable. However, you maintain full control over your data through your device settings.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

As all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights through your device settings or by deleting the App.

Children's Privacy

NutriSlider is not intended for users under 18 years of age without parent/guardian consultation. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe such information has been inadvertently collected, please contact me to have it removed.

Your Privacy Controls

You can:

  • Clear all stored data by deleting the App
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
  • Control App permissions through your device settings
  • Purchase the premium version to remove advertisements

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Information

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or NutriSlider's privacy practices, please contact me at: support@nutrislider.com
